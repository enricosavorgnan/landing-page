What we offer

Our app helps children develop effective mental habits through Habits of Mind—dispositions that foster curiosity, perseverance, and mindful thinking—and the CASEL framework, an international standard for social-emotional skills development and assessment.

With LiteAI, children learn to...

Persevere in the face of obstacles without giving up easily

Ask questions with curiosity, fueling their desire to learn

Manage emotions constructively, without being overwhelmed by them

Listen with empathy, understanding others’ points of view

Collaborate, developing a sense of shared responsibility

Think creatively, finding original solutions to problems

Reflect on their thinking, striving for continuous improvement

Stay flexible, adapting to new situations

What exactly are the Habits of Mind?

They are 16 mental dispositions that help children face challenges with curiosity, resilience, and critical thinking.
They are not facts to memorize, but true tools for learning, collaborating, and growing.

We train reflective thinking and the ability to learn

We support children in developing mental strategies that help them reflect on their actions and improve themselves continuously.
This approach makes them more self-aware, autonomous, and prepared to face new challenges.

We monitor emotional and relational growth

We know that play is a powerful educational lever. Through mazes, treasure hunts, and racetrack sprints, LiteAI offers structured activities based on the Habits of Mind that spark curiosity, perseverance, and reflective thinking. Every activity is developed with a team of pedagogues to ensure educational rigor and learning continuity.

We assess abilities like empathy, self-control, and collaboration

Each child has a specific cognitive and emotional profile.
Our app identifies which mental dispositions—Habits of Mind—to work on and personalizes the learning path with targeted activities.
The artificial intelligence selects the most suitable activities, based on the child's development and individual needs.

We provide personalized and clear feedback for parents and educators

LiteAI is designed to support these key figures in the delicate task of children's education.
That’s why we’ve created a specific section for adults, with periodic reports and feedback on the child’s progress.
We have also developed activities that encourage connection and interaction between adults and children.
